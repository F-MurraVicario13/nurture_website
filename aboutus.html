Our Mission

At NurTree, we believe that children's education should be engaging, effective, and accessible. Our mission is to revolutionize the time and effort parents spend trying to educate their children by providing a comprehensive educational and screen-time management application.

We're dedicated to creating a nurturing environment where children can grow and learn while giving parents the tools they need to manage screen time effectively.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at NurTree

Education First

We believe that quality education is a right for every child. Our content is designed with educational best practices at its core, ensuring children learn while they play.

Family Focused

We design our app with the entire family in mind. NurTree empowers parents with tools to guide their children's digital experience while fostering meaningful family interactions.

Digital Wellness

We promote healthy digital habits by creating a balanced approach to screen time that prioritizes quality content and encourages breaks and diverse activities.
